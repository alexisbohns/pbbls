## Intellectual inspirations

Pebbles would not exist without the work of the following people, who have deeply influenced the product's design:

**Cognitive science and behavioral psychology:** Daniel Kahneman (thinking systems), Aaron Beck (cognitive behavioral therapy), Abraham Maslow (hierarchy of needs), Lisa Feldman Barrett (emotion construction), Wendy Suzuki (neuroplasticity and habits), Anna Lembke (dopamine and balance).

**Behavioral design and habits:** James Clear (Atomic Habits), BJ Fogg (Tiny Habits), Nir Eyal (Hooked Model), Edward Deci and Richard Ryan (Self-Determination Theory).

**Game design:** Will Wright (The Sims — simulating everyday life), Sid Meier (Civilization — the "one more turn" engagement mechanic).

**Product and methods:** The teams at Linear, Notion, Figma, and Supabase, whose product-building methods are a continuous source of inspiration.

**Applications:** Duolingo (ethical gamification of habits), Polarsteps (turning a trip into a story), Day One (private journaling and personal archive).

## Standards

**Apple HealthKit** — Pebbles' mood and emotion data model is compatible with Apple's `HKStateOfMind` standards, enabling future interoperability with the health ecosystem.

**GDPR and CNIL** — Pebbles' privacy practices follow the General Data Protection Regulation and the guidelines of the French Data Protection Authority.
