## Preamble

Pebbles is an application designed to help you collect and revisit your daily memories, in complete peace of mind. We believe that a good privacy policy is fundamentally an act of respect toward our users. This document explains, clearly and transparently, what data we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and what rights you have.

Pebbles functions as a personal and local application. We do not profile your emotions for advertising. We do not share your memories anywhere. Your data remains yours.

This privacy policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the guidelines of the French Data Protection Authority (CNIL).

## 2. Data Collected

### 2.1 Data Provided Directly by You

When you sign up and use Pebbles, you voluntarily provide us with the following data:

- **Account Data :** Your full name, email address, and avatar (profile photo).
- **Events :** Your free-text responses to structured questions (situation, emotions, thoughts, reactions, learnings, feedback) inspired by Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT).
- **Moods :** The type of mood recorded (daily mood or momentary emotion), your rating on a 7-point scale, up to 38 emotion labels, up to 18 associations with life domains, and intensity and impact scores.
- **Decisions :** Your evaluations of emotional impact and associations (scored from -3 to +3).
- **Souls :** The first names or names of people you associate with your events (third-party data you enter yourself).
- **Therapist Relationship :** If applicable, the access permissions you grant to your therapist.

### 2.2 Data Generated by Your Usage

When you use Pebbles, we automatically generate:

- **Bounce Karma :** A metric indicating your regularity of engagement with the app.
- **Achievements and Progress :** The badges and milestones you unlock.
- **Cairns :** Weekly and monthly aggregations of your data.

### 2.3 Technical Data

To operate and ensure your security, we process:

- **Authentication Logs :** The timestamps of your sign-ups, logins, and logouts (via Supabase audit logs).
- **Session Tokens :** The session cookies provided by Supabase to maintain your login.
- **Standard HTTP Headers :** Technical metadata about your device and browser.

### 2.4 Third-Party Processing

- **Google Gemma :** We use Google Gemma (a language model) to enhance certain features. Only anonymized event data is sent to Google. No personal identifiers (name, email, soul names) are transmitted.
- **Fonts :** We use self-hosted fonts (Ysabeau OFL and system-ui). No external requests are made to font CDNs.

### 2.5 Third-Party Personal Data

If you record the names of people dear to you (your "souls"), these names are stored in your Pebbles account. Pebbles never contacts these people and never shares their data with them.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

In accordance with Article 6 of the GDPR, we process your data on the following legal bases:

### 3.1 Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b))

Processing of the following data is necessary to perform the Pebbles service agreement:
- Account data (registration, identification)
- Events, moods, and responses (recording and storage)
- Bounce karma and achievements (core functionality)

### 3.2 Explicit Consent (Art. 9(2)(a))

The following data constitutes special category personal data within the meaning of Article 9 of the GDPR and is processed only with your explicit consent, which you provide during onboarding:
- Moods and emotion labels (qualified as health data)
- CBT reflections and personal analysis
- Social sharing (if enabled)
- Therapist access (if applicable)

You may withdraw your consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

### 3.3 Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f))

We use your data on the basis of our legitimate interests for:
- **Security :** Fraud prevention, authentication, and security maintenance (authentication logs).
- **Product Improvement :** Analysis of anonymized aggregates to enhance user experience.

### 3.4 Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))

We process your data if required by law, including in response to judicial or administrative requests.

## 4. Special Category Data and Health Data

### 4.1 Qualification

The moods, emotions, and CBT reflections you record in Pebbles may be qualified as special category data within the meaning of Article 9 of the GDPR (data concerning mental health and psychological well-being).

### 4.2 Processing and Consent

This data is processed only:
- On the basis of your explicit consent, obtained during onboarding;
- With your full control over who accesses it (you alone, unless you grant access to a therapist);
- Solely for purposes of personal improvement, memory collection, and, if applicable, therapeutic support.

### 4.3 Right to Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw your consent at any time via your profile. Withdrawal of consent does not render prior processing unlawful.

### 4.4 HealthKit (if applicable)

If you authorize Pebbles to access health data via HealthKit (Apple):
- This data will never be used for advertising purposes.
- It will never be shared with third parties without your consent.
- You can revoke access at any time in Apple's settings.

## 5. Therapist Access and Shared Data

### 5.1 Access Control

If you work with a therapist, you may share your Pebbles account via granular permissions:
- `can_view_general` : Access to general statistics and trends.
- `can_view_events` : Access to your detailed events, moods, and reflections.

### 5.2 You Remain Responsible

- You grant, modify, and revoke these permissions at any time via your profile.
- Your therapist is an independent data controller for their therapeutic practice. Pebbles is not responsible for how your therapist uses your data.
- Sharing is entirely optional.

### 5.3 Right of Revocation

You may revoke therapist access at any time without affecting your Pebbles account.

Pebbles is intended for users aged 13 and above. In France, the digital age of consent is set at 15 years by CNIL, but we accept users from age 13.

### 10.2 Parental Consent (Ages 13-14)

If you are between 13 and 14 years old, using Pebbles requires prior consent from your parents or legal guardians. We ask you to confirm this consent during sign-up.

## 11. Data Security

### 11.1 Technical Measures

- **Row-Level Security (RLS) :** Each user can only access their own data via Supabase security policies.
- **Authentication :** Secure authentication via Supabase Auth with email and password.
- **Encryption in Transit :** All connections use HTTPS.
- **Encryption at Rest :** Data is encrypted at rest via Supabase infrastructure.

### 11.2 Organizational Measures

- Pebbles is managed by a single developer, Alexis Bohn, who follows security best practices.
- Limited access to data: only the data controller has access to systems.
- No permanent audit or user data logging is retained without necessity.

### 11.3 User Responsibility

You are responsible for keeping your password confidential. Never share your login credentials. If you believe your account is compromised, contact hello@bohns.design immediately.

## 12. AI Features and Anonymized Data

### 12.1 Use of Google Gemma

If you enable AI features, Pebbles processes your events with Google Gemma to generate suggestions or analyses.

### 12.2 Anonymization

Before transmission to Google Gemma, the following data is removed or anonymized:
- Your name, email, and account identifiers.
- Names of "souls" (associated people).
- Any metadata that could identify you.

Only the event text and associated emotions are processed.

### 12.3 Your Responsibility

If you include personal information directly in your event text (people's names, precise locations, identifiers), you accept that this information may be transmitted to Google Gemma. We recommend using your "souls" for people's names rather than writing them plainly.

### 12.4 Disabling AI Features

AI features are entirely optional. You can disable them at any time via your settings.
