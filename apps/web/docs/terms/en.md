## 1. Definitions

### 1.1 Key Terms

- **Pebble**: A memory, event, or moment recorded by a Collector, enriched with emotional data, reflections inspired by cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques, and connections to Souls.

- **Soul**: A person linked to a Collector's Pebbles, representing someone in the Collector's life (friend, family, colleague, etc.).

- **Collector**: A Pebbles user who records and revisits memories.

- **Emotion Pearl**: A nuanced emotional shape attached to a Pebble, enabling deeper exploration of emotional states.

- **Bounce / Karma**: An engagement system measuring the regularity of visits and interactions with Pebbles, without constituting a "streak" or contractual obligation.

- **Cairn**: A weekly or monthly summary grouping multiple Pebbles into a personal collection.

- **Collection**: A curated set of Pebbles grouped around a theme, period, or intention.

- **Domain**: A life domain inspired by the revisited Maslow pyramid, used to categorize Pebbles (health, relationships, creativity, learning, etc.).

- **User Account**: Your personal and secure access to the platform.

- **User Content**: All data created or uploaded by the Collector, including Pebbles, Souls, Collections, notes, and preferences.
